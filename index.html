Luke Arnold

Freelance Designer and Prototype Engineer

Luke integrates engineering, industrial design, and advanced prototyping, with a focus on consumer goods. Many design teams have benefited from his technical skills and creative solutions throughout the product development process.

Known as a designer with a resourceful nature, creative approach to engineering with a deep knowledge of modern prototyping and manufacturing methods.

Luke has worked with large brands Loewe, Rituals, and Vyrao to develop innovative eco-friendly packaging. His luxury moulded-fibre packaging solutions have been shipped globally and mass-produced up to 1 million units.

Luke has recently developed a new consumer electronic device for Japan Tobacco International as part of a small team at Conju Space. His work prototyping and designing the product has helped secure funding to launch the product globally.
